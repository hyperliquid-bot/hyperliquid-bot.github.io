Hyperliquid Bot FAQ

What is a Hyperliquid bot?

A Hyperliquid bot is software that trades for you automatically on Hyperliquid, the high-performance perpetuals DEX. Instead of placing every order by hand, the bot runs a strategy — AI-driven or rule-based — on your chosen pairs, around the clock, from your own account.

Is a Hyperliquid bot safe to use?

Safety comes down to control and permissions. A good Hyperliquid bot trades from your own account using a trading key, without the ability to withdraw your funds, and you review and can stop it anytime. Never share your seed phrase, and perps carry market and liquidation risk regardless of the tool.

How do I connect a bot to Hyperliquid?

You authorize the bot with a Hyperliquid trading/agent key (not your withdrawal keys), choose a pair and strategy, set your budget, and enable it. Hyperliquid Bot then trades that allocation automatically while you keep control of the account.

Can a Hyperliquid bot trade perps?

Yes — Hyperliquid is primarily a perpetuals DEX, so trading perps is the core use. Hyperliquid Bot can run perp strategies like trend, grid, funding-rate and copy-trading, as well as spot where available.

Does a Hyperliquid bot run 24/7?

Yes. Crypto markets never close, so the bot keeps executing your strategy day and night — reacting to conditions while you are away. You can pause or adjust it at any time.

Do I keep control of my funds?

Yes. Hyperliquid Bot is built to trade from your own Hyperliquid account on a budget you set; it executes trades but does not take custody of your funds, and you can stop it whenever you like.
