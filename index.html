FAQ

Hyperliquid Bot questions

01What is Hyperliquid Bot?

Hyperliquid Bot is a non-custodial DeFi checker for traders reviewing bot-style workflows around Hyperliquid markets. It helps you inspect wallet setup, market intent, collateral context and final app prompts before action.

02Does Hyperliquid Bot take custody of funds?

No. The experience is wallet-native: you connect your own wallet, keep your keys and review the prompts you sign. The checker does not ask you to deposit funds into a separate custodian.

03Which chains and assets are supported?

The page focuses on Hyperliquid, HyperEVM context, HYPE and USDC workflows. Current routes and availability can change, so use the supported chains page and the app for the live list.

04How long does a bot check take?

The read-only preview is immediate on the page, while live quotes, wallet prompts and any on-chain or trading actions depend on current network and market conditions shown in the app.

05What does it cost to use?

The page does not quote fixed fees because trading costs, funding, spreads and network costs can change. Review the live cost details in the app before you sign or submit anything.

06Is a bot safer than manual trading?

A bot can make execution more consistent, but it does not remove market risk. Treat permissions, strategy settings and wallet prompts as serious controls, and use the checker before every material change.
