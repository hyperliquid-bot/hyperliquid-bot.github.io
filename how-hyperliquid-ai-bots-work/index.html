Quick answer

A Hyperliquid AI bot is third-party software that trades for you on Hyperliquid using an automated, AI-assisted strategy. You authorize it with a Hyperliquid API (agent) wallet — it can trade but not withdraw — pick a strategy and budget, and it runs 24/7 while you keep control of your account. Hyperliquid Bot is independent and not an official Hyperliquid product.

What is a Hyperliquid AI bot?

It's a third-party tool that automates trading on Hyperliquid — the bots are not built by Hyperliquid itself.

Hyperliquid is a high-performance on-chain exchange (an L1 with order books for perpetuals and spot). It provides the markets, order types and an API, but it does not ship an official bot. A "Hyperliquid AI bot" is software that connects to that API and trades for you. You can confirm what's native versus third-party in the Hyperliquid documentation. Hyperliquid Bot is one such independent tool.

How does a Hyperliquid AI bot work?

It connects through an API wallet, reads the market, and places orders on your behalf according to a strategy — continuously.

You authorize the bot with a Hyperliquid API (agent) wallet, which can sign trades but cannot move your funds out. The bot then watches price and conditions, and executes your strategy — opening, managing and closing positions on the pairs you chose. Because it's software, it reacts in moments and runs through the night, which is the whole point of automating perps.

AI bot vs rule-based bot — what's the difference?

A rule-based bot follows fixed rules; an "AI" bot adds adaptive logic on top — both automate the execution for you.

Rule-based botAI bot
DecisionsFixed conditions you setAdaptive / signal-assisted logic

Either way, the bot handles execution and discipline; you decide how much it can trade and can stop it anytime.

What a Hyperliquid bot can't do

It automates trading, but it does not remove the risks of leveraged perpetuals.

Hyperliquid's core market is perpetual futures — leveraged products that carry liquidation, funding and market risk. A bot can be disciplined and fast, but it cannot guarantee profit, and "guaranteed returns" is a red flag anywhere in crypto. Read Hyperliquid's own risk overview before you trade with leverage, automated or not.

What do you need to run one?

A Hyperliquid account, an API wallet, a strategy and a budget you're comfortable with.

Frequently asked questions

Is there an official Hyperliquid AI bot?

No. Hyperliquid provides the exchange, order types and an API, but the bots are third-party tools that connect to it. Hyperliquid Bot is an independent automated-trading tool and is not affiliated with Hyperliquid.

Does a Hyperliquid bot need my seed phrase?

No. A Hyperliquid bot should connect through an API (agent) wallet that can place trades but cannot withdraw your funds. Never share your seed phrase with any bot — a legitimate one never asks for it.

Can a Hyperliquid AI bot guarantee profit?

No. Hyperliquid trades perpetual futures, which are leveraged and carry liquidation, funding and market risk. A bot automates execution and discipline; it does not remove risk or guarantee returns.

Does a Hyperliquid AI bot work for perps?

Yes — perpetuals are Hyperliquid's core market, so perps are the main use. A bot can run perp strategies like trend, grid, funding-rate and copy-trading on the pairs you choose.
